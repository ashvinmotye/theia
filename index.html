What's it about?

Theia offers text-to-speech conversion to assist visually impaired and blind persons alike the possibility to read their mails and perform other daily tasks with great ease and comfort. Hence bridging the gap to the employment world!

FAST

Theia offers amazing processing speed. Samples of over 500 words get processed in only 0.9s.

ACCURATE

Tested with over 1000 samples, Theia maintains an accurate conversion rate of 97%.

EFFICIENT

Low memory usgae and low battery consumption makes Theia really stands out from competition!
